# LifeLine Submission Notes

## Elevator Pitch

LifeLine is a mobile emergency response app that turns a phone or wearable alert into a real-time SOS, shares critical medical info, and routes the fastest nearby trained responder before an ambulance arrives.

## Demo Flow

1. Sign in as a citizen and complete the medical profile.
2. Add at least one family contact and one medical provider.
3. Trigger an emergency from the home screen with manual SOS or the wearable simulation.
4. Sign in on a second device or simulator as a responder.
5. Approve the responder for demo, set availability, and choose travel mode.
6. Accept the incoming alert, mark arrival, request backup if needed, and complete the assignment.

## Standout Points

- Dual experience for both citizens and responders in the same app
- Wearable-style trigger simulation with vital signs context
- Real-time Convex incident state and timeline logging
- ETA-based responder ranking instead of simple nearest-distance matching
- Emergency medical profile sharing with privacy controls

## Judging Framing

LifeLine focuses on the minutes before professional emergency services arrive. The prototype demonstrates how a phone or wearable can escalate an incident, attach useful medical context, and route the fastest verified nearby helper in real time.
